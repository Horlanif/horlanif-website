History of Horlanif Enterprise

Horlanif Enterprise was founded in 2016 by Muftau Saheed Olamiposi, prior to his admission into the university. The business started with the importation and sales of memory cards, flash drives, and ear pods, serving customers in Osogbo, Osun State. Upon admission into the university, the business expanded to Ikole and Oye Ekiti, where he added online registration services, assisting students with school payments and academic registrations. This strengthened the business presence within the student community. During his NYSC year in Ibadan, Horlanif Enterprise reached a wider audience, extending its customer base across Oyo State.
Over the years, the brand diversified its offerings, moving beyond gadgets to:
Phones and Power Banks
Phone Holders and USB Bulbs
Electronic Gadgets Kitchen Appliances
Quality Customized Jerseys (Football & Basketball)
In addition, the company offers professional services such as pre-order training, thesis/project writing, online registration, graphic design, and web design. The brand name Horlanif was creatively formed from two names: Olamiposi and Nifemi (the founder’s names), symbolizing a blend of personal identity and vision.
As a graduate of Agricultural Engineering, with a keen interest in smart farming and irrigation systems, Muftau Saheed Olamiposi also envisions expanding Horlanif Enterprise into the agriculture sector. His goal is to Integrate modern technology with farming practices, contributing to food security and sustainable agriculture in Nigeria. Today, Horlanif Enterprise has grown from a small gadget business into a multi-service enterprise with operations covering the entire South-West region of Nigeria, and a customer base spread across the entire country. Guided by innovation, trust, and customer satisfaction, the company continues to pursue its vision of being one of Nigeria’s most reliable and diversified brands.
